Our Story

Built on Passion, Driven by Expertise

Higher Ground Landscaping was founded in 1996 by Tony Barash while attending Michigan State University. What started as a one-man operation fueled by a genuine love for transforming outdoor spaces has grown into Oakland County's most trusted landscape contractor.

For nearly three decades, Tony and his team have been solving Michigan's toughest landscape challenges — from complex drainage problems that other contractors won't touch, to creating stunning outdoor living spaces that extend the joy of homeownership.

Today, Higher Ground Landscaping is known throughout Birmingham, Bloomfield Hills, Troy, Rochester, Royal Oak, and all of Oakland County for expert craftsmanship, honest pricing, and a genuine commitment to every client's vision.

Meet the Owner

Tony Barash — Oakland County's Foremost Drainage Expert & Landscape Contractor

Tony Barash brings nearly 30 years of hands-on experience to every project. He personally meets with every client, assesses every property, and oversees every installation. When you hire Higher Ground, you're hiring Tony's brain — his deep understanding of Michigan soils, drainage patterns, freeze-thaw cycles, and the materials that withstand them.

Tony is known for his honest approach, creative problem-solving, and willingness to work within any budget. He doesn't do high-pressure sales. He listens to what you want, tells you what you need, and delivers results that exceed expectations.
