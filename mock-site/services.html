Core Service

Yard Drainage Solutions

Standing water, soggy lawns, and foundation damage are common problems in Michigan. Higher Ground Landscaping specializes in comprehensive drainage solutions that protect your property and restore your yard to usable condition. We are Oakland County's #1 ranked drainage contractor.

Tony Barash has been solving Michigan's toughest drainage problems since 1996. We don't just install drains — we engineer complete water management systems that account for Michigan's freeze-thaw cycles, clay soils, and heavy spring runoff.

  • French Drain Installation & Repair
  • Catch Basin Systems
  • Site Grading & Re-grading
  • Foundation Drainage
  • Underground Downspout Extensions
  • Sump Pump Discharge Lines
  • Channel Drains (Driveway & Patio)
  • Dry Creek Beds & Swales

Core Service

Brick & Stone Paver Installation

Transform your outdoor spaces with premium brick and stone pavers. From elegant patios and driveways to custom walkways and pool decks, our paver installations are built to last decades in Michigan's demanding climate.

We use only premium materials with proper base preparation — 6-8" compacted gravel base, geotextile fabric, and polymeric sand — ensuring your pavers stay level and beautiful through years of Michigan winters.

  • Paver Patios & Outdoor Entertainment Areas
  • Paver Driveways & Motor Courts
  • Walkways & Garden Paths
  • Pool Deck Paver Installation
  • Paver Steps & Staircases
  • Raised & Elevated Paver Patios
  • Paver Repair & Restoration
  • Bluestone, Flagstone & Natural Stone

Structural Service

Retaining Walls

Engineered retaining walls solve erosion problems, create usable tiered spaces, and add dramatic visual impact to your property. We build walls that are structurally sound and visually stunning.

Every retaining wall we build includes proper drainage behind the wall, compacted gravel base, and geogrid reinforcement for walls over 3 feet. We use natural stone, boulders, and segmental block systems.

  • Natural Stone Retaining Walls
  • Boulder Retaining Walls
  • Segmental Block Walls
  • Terraced Garden Walls
  • Erosion Control Solutions
  • Wall Drainage Integration
  • Decorative Garden Walls
